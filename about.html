Get to Know Me ✨

Hi, I'm Lilia. It's nice to meet you!

I am a 2018 graduate from UC San Diego, where I studied Cognitive Science with Specialization in Human Computer Interaction. My background in user centered design allows me to create solutions for products that will impact people’s lives in a meaningful way.

I strive to understand the root of design challenges and what users need to achieve their goals seamlessly. My design process is all about being an empathetic problem solver backed by research and remaining user-focused.

In my free time, I enjoy playing my acoustic guitar, figure skating, keeping up with the beauty and fashion industries, and walking around San Francisco to find new brunch spots.

I am currently looking for full-time opportunities in User Experience where I can contribute to design projects and grow as a designer. Feel free to check out my Resumé for more information.
